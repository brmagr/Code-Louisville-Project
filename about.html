About Us

Since 1953 great seafood and Hungry Pelican have become synonymous in the Kentuckiana area.

Originally called The Cape Codder we took on the Hungry Pelican name and logo in 1978. We've built a proud reputation for excellence by offering abundant variety, friendly service and especially reasonable prices in our Dining Room and Carry Out. A reputation the Hungry Pelican Family works hard to maintain with each and every item we serve, 7 days a week to good folks like you. Welcome Aboard!
